About

The UK CBD vape market is crowded with weak, CBD-only products dressed up as premium — expensive formulas that don’t deliver. Canna Cubed was built for the modern urban lifestyle, engineered for people moving through late nights, early mornings, and everything in between. Instead of relying on CBD alone, we use a balanced cannabinoid blend of CBD, CBG and CBC, each chosen for how it supports everyday wellbeing. CBD forms the foundation for calm and balance, CBG adds structure and clarity for focus and mental sharpness, and CBC complements the blend to support a more rounded, consistent experience. Designed and manufactured in the UK, Canna Cubed CBD vapes are discreet, refined, and made for the fast pace of modern life — minimal design, intentional formulation, and a presence that doesn’t need to shout.
